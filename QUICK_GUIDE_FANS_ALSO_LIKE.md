# 🎤 Quick Guide: "Fans Also Like" Feature

## What You'll See

After the song recommendations on your Home page, you'll now see a new section:

```
🎤 Fans also like

[●]  [●]  [●]  [●]  [●]  [●]  [●]  [●]
Artist Artist Artist Artist Artist Artist Artist Artist
```

Each circle shows:
- Artist profile picture (circular)
- Artist name below
- "Artist" label

## How to Use

1. **Scroll down** on Home page after recommendations
2. **Hover over an artist** - See smooth animation and glow effect
3. **Click an artist** - Automatically search for their songs
4. **Discover new music** from similar artists

## Smart Recommendations

The system shows artists based on:
- ✅ Artists from your liked songs
- ✅ Artists from your listening history
- ✅ Trending Indian artists (if you're new)

## Design Features

- 🎨 Circular profile images (like Spotify)
- ✨ Purple gradient hover effects
- 📱 Mobile responsive (smaller on phones)
- 🎭 Smooth animations
- 🌙 Dark glassmorphism design
